My Favorite Application

Adobe Photoshop Lightroom

Lightroom is a photo editing app. It is free and accessible to almost all platforms and it's famous for editing. Some friends and professionals recommend it since it is both user-friendly and for those with experience.

I like doing travel photography, portrait, candid and lifestyle.

Lightroom is good because you have the freedom to edit the photo in however you like and if you are kind of not into editing however you like there are already presets. Also, when you are editing, I think there is an option that auto saves so you get back to the picture you were editing.

I use it when I take photos of something good but lacks color or shadow, I have been using it for two years but not so often though.

Most features are to be paid, so I use basic features mostly the editing and saving pictures straight to your devices. you can create albums easily too.
