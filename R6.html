R6

Color Theory and Typography

Activity Reflection

The activity was to watch the 2 YouTube video links from below. Then play the succeeding 4 games. Take a screenshot of each screen/score and compile in one document to be submitted as your A6. It was a simple task and an intriguing one since the activity tell us to play a game that can help us in our design thinking. For me, I was always ready for those kinds of activity and I find it interesting as well to test myself on design games.

First was to watch the videos, the first video was in-depth but really tells us that we must make designs clear for people not to misjudge or see it in the wrong way since it may look incorrect to some. There are consequences with bad graphic design, and it is something that everyone should be aware of. The second video I thought fonts are always there to make it easier to read, but typefaces are also used for something for it to describe and make it easier what that typeface wants us to imagine. Cooper Black was used mostly information and its design is amusing for most people. I now realize even the slightest curves on the letters of that typeface would make such a difference and even if it was bold or medium.

Finally, I entered the first game and played it. I was fairly good at the Kerning type and only a few times I think I made a slight space error on each since a longer letter required me more focus. It was interesting to see how spaces need to be perfectly matched with each letter and made me realize that designing words are hard and I applaud to those designers who do this on the hour.

I then played Brandseen, I was having both a hard time and an OK time with this since some of them are easy to do. I didnâ€™t know the Apple color background and that part is where I failed. It is also interesting to see how specific the colors are for each application of logo and it really is appealing to create that perfect color combo.

I played Better Web Type which I think was the most hardest game for me. I did not know how to display the sentences correctly since I really have no idea unlike the other two which was quite explanatory. I only got a few correct on this game and I think I need to improve and see how sentences are made with its spacing or how long the sentence should be.

Lastly, I played the Type War where we had typefaces and had to choose the correct typeface. I was quite impressed with myself to distinguish the different typefaces and I liked this game a lot. I think that the game is a good way to introduce designers to typefaces and how they would look like.
