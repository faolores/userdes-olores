R1

My First Few Weeks of USERDES Class

Over the past month in USERDES class I started to learn more about user design since I like projects that are exciting and pleasing in the eye made by me. I had two questions to reflect my work, thinking process, and even a career in the future. How did USERDES class affected my idea and how I usually think and what I realized in the past month. I’d like to relay my thoughts on this paper so that I may find a reason on why my questions are important.

Week 1

When the first few activities such as my favorite application and creation of website, I needed to make this pleasing for me and for the people who will view it. I was conscious of my work and I wanted to work hard on it as if it could help me in my career and can serve as part of my portfolio. Every time there are projects that could be related to my course, I'd like to do my best since these are what will help me once I graduate. To be honest, when I was thinking of my favorite application, there were social media apps that I could’ve mentioned but I wanted to show an application that portrays my hobbies. For my website, I wanted it to be simple but straight-forward just like a resume. Since there is freedom and no right or wrong answers for my questions, I wanted to be as honest as possible and just say that it wasn’t stressful but comforting because I am able to do as I please on my work and projects.

Week 2

I realized in the past month that UI and UX are important topics in my course, anything with technical work always has a feedback of the customer or user. I also realized that all of us started out as users, therefore, if we become the people that takes care of the experience of our apps’ users then we should also do some reflection to further develop the relationship of designer and user.

Week 3

During the process, I learned that it’s harder for my professor to help us in designing our own projects since we are on lockdown but instead, she still gives us feedback which is the most important. I am confident that the students also know that fact and we still try our best. Nevertheless, we like that on our projects, we have freedom to create however we want.
