Intro to Design Thinking Process

The General Idea of Design Thinking

Design thinking for me was quite amazing, since it involved a group which you will have to brainstorm and come up with creative ideas individually and having group discussions is always something I longed for during the pandemic. For me, having my idea picked is exhilarating, knowing that the course of the project is up to me since it is my idea and also knowing that my groupmates approve of it as well. When I was learning design thinking, instead of students, how would those with experience or those who do these types of job everyday think? It is quite intriguing since those that are professionals are probably fast and know their team very well. Anyways, Design Thinking for me is like everyday life, discovering problems and the needs, then coming up with an idea how to do it, it is like finishing tasks in education, but creatively and is organized.

The Design Thinking Process

I find myself sympathizing and analyzing with my works in USERDES, I always tried to figure out who my audience is when writing or creating, then I try to see it from their perspective and then think about what else could improve on, then finally, I try to create it and modify it in according to their feedback.

Why I thought Design Thinking is Important

So, similar to what I said my work on G2, people have different thoughts, ideas, hardships, and even experiences. Like how I saw my classmates have different problems on their midterm presentation, those are what composes of Design Thinking and it is the product of everyone that made it happen. Yes, there are times when similar ideas come to mind in between individuals but that is what makes Design Thinking exciting, being on both terms and collaborating to create a single but strong idea.
