Invitation Design

Instructions

1. How can we improve this?

You can point out details and give comments for improvements

We can improve the given invitation by having the sentences split and organized than a whole paragraph. I would arrange and design the draft like this:

You are invited to the (*removed cordially so that it is straight to the point.)

After dinner party (*also delectable is removed same reason with above.)

Robert and Alexandra (*In big text.)

Saturday, 30th of June (added “Saturday” so that it isn’t confusing.)

9:30 in the evening (instead of pm.)

Wine and snacks (*changed nibble to snacks since I think nibble is a word that is acted.)

Address (*a vague address was given but there should always be an address in invitation cards.)

RSVP (*should include your number; we don’t need long sentence for them to let us know.)

I’d like to add that on the last sentence, “Kindly let us know if you’ll be attending by june 1st is wrong since his invitation date is not june 1st but instead june 30th So that should be replaced with RSVP instead.

2. What would be the objective function?

Tell me what the passage is all about. What is the objective?

The purpose of the passage is to invite selected people as it is an invitation, the passage also tells us that it is Robert and Alexandra’s after-dinner party and that wine and snacks will be served on the after-dinner party at the day of June 30th, 2012 at 9:30pm. It will be held at the pad and if directions are needed, just message Robert and Alexandra. Then they ask to let the guests tell them if they will be attending.

The objective of the invitation is to know who will be attending the after-dinner party through a ping or message. Also, to fully make sure the guests understand where they will go, what time the event is, and they will be having and expecting on the event.
