Week 1 (September 12)

On our first week, I chose Aires and Clifford to be my group members, I knew they were fast thinkers and creative. So, if I remember It was only me and Aires at that time and we tried to just brainstorm and throw ideas whatever comes to mind for the problem that we are facing right now, in the middle of discussing that, I liked some of his ideas and so did he. In the end we tried to focus on problem topics that could be easier for us such as the exercising during pandemic, earning money, and diet. We chose earning money since it is quite controversial between students and even people who are at quarantine. After the problem, we tried to have the possible quotes, pain points and goals on the same day to have progress on the content. Week 2 (September 19)

So, it was our first presentation, and I was kind of in between of being confident and nervous at the same time since my groupmates had trouble with connection and internet so I had to present by myself. But I felt good since It was an idea made by my groupmates. I think I did well on the presentation, but our content lacks a bit, but It was fine since there was always a next time and I thought to myself, this time we could correct the mistakes and come back with a better presentation since my team knew what needed to change. I also felt like being a proper leader since when I ask them to do something, they ask or sometimes I give examples for that sector of the presentation.

Week 3 (September 26)

It was an asynchronous session. but a group was to give feedbacks, comments, or suggestion to our updated presentation from Week 2. I actually liked being criticized for our work since it only gives us a chance to make the output better than usual. We also gave our opinions and what we liked about their presentation and what needed to change and surprisingly my team and I gave good feedbacks for them and made us felt like we were doing a good job to help them get better next time.
